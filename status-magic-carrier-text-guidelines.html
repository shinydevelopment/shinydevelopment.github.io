Custom Carrier Text on App Store Screenshots

Apple publish a set of App Store Marketing Guidelines which give plenty of guidance on marketing your apps, including the preparation of assets for the App Store.

The "Custom Photography and Video" section of the document states:

If the status bar is clearly visible, make sure that the battery and Wi-Fi icons are full and that no carrier name is displayed.

We would agree with Apple that if Status Magic is being used to prepare screenshots for the App Store then the option to add custom carrier text should not be used.

So why has this feature been added? Well, we originally designed this app purely for App Store screenshot preparation but we are aware that people are now using Status Magic for various other purposes and the feature has been added to support those users.
