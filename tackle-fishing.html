Tackle Fishing

Tackle Fishing is the app for anyone who wants to improve their coarse fishing techniques and catch more fish.

Take this app with you next time you go fishing and see results with these easy to follow rigs and techniques.

Buy Tackle Fishing Now!

Features

Aimed at the beginner/intermediate angler and stunningly illustrated throughout, the app contains beautiful cross sections with amazing detail and depth covering lakes & fisheries, canals and rivers identifying the key locations to fish. For each location a detailed rig diagrams show the float, weights, bait and hook you need. Pro tips help improve your technique further and increase your catch.

With a great selection of tried and tested rigs, sumptuous graphics plus a really useful section on Plumbing the Depths and an Essential Buying Guide, this app is an invaluable aid for the keen angler.

About Chris Mayer

The rigs and content have been developed and modernised by Chris Mayer, who has coached fishing in America as well as at home in England. Chris is an ex-England Youth International and who was taught his fishing skills by his late Father, Alan Mayer, who won over 200 Open Fishing Competitions as well as individual winner of the Division 1 National Championships and a member of the England Senior Team.
